Milestones in India

A visual timeline of Pallottine presence and growth in India.

Timeline

75 years of Pallottine presence

1951

The first two German missionaries arrived in Bombay and proceeded to Nagpur and Raipur.

1955

Foundation of the Christsevikas of the Society of the Catholic Apostolate.

1956

First novitiate house for Indian Pallottines was established in Raipur.

1957

The first Pallottine school in India, Holy Cross School, Byron Bazar, Raipur.

1959

First Indian priest was ordained and the first Indian Pallottine brother made profession.

1961

The Pallottine Delegature was officially established in India.

1964

Msgr. Hans Weidner was appointed first Prefect Apostolic of Raipur.

1981

Pallottigiri was established as a formation centre.

1988

The Pallottines established Northern Delegature.
