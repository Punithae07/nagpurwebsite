Vision & Mission

Revive faith and enkindle charity

Vision

To revive faith and enkindle charity in the entire world, collaborating with all people of good will to spread the Gospel of Christ.

Mission

To empower every member of the Church to become an apostle in their own sphere of life, fostering unity and renewal.
