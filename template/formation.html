List of Formation Centres

Formation houses and study centres

Pallottigiri

Vocation Orientation Centre for Kerala, North and North East of India, serving as an important early stage of discernment and accompaniment.

Casa Pallotti

Minor seminary in Umran, Shillong, dedicated to the initial formation of students from the Northeast in a setting of prayer, study and community living.

Pallotti Bhawan

A major house of formation at Seminary Hills, Nagpur, supporting candidates through a disciplined life of fraternity, study and spiritual growth.

Pradeepa

Pallotti Institute of Philosophy and Religion situated in Goa, where philosophical and religious formation deepens intellectual and pastoral readiness.

Prabodhana

Pallottine Theologate at Mysore for theological and religious formation, preparing students for deeper service to the Church and the Society.
